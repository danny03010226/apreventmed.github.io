Engineering challenges in advanced packaging

As package size and power density increase, packaging performance becomes limited by structural and thermo-mechanical constraints.

  • Substrate warpage driven by CTE mismatch and thermal gradients
  • Heat spreading limitations affecting junction temperature and margin
  • Mechanical instability during assembly and reliability testing
  • Trade-off between thermal performance and structural stiffness

  • DFM-oriented guidance (flatness, tolerance, interfaces)

Why AlSiC

  • Tunable CTE to align silicon/substrate/mechanical parts
  • High thermal conductivity for lateral spreading
  • High modulus for structural stability
  • Lower density than CuW-based materials
